Movie Recommendations by Aleena

My Top 3 Movies/Anime of all time.

1. When Marnie was There

This is my favourite movie by Ghibli Studio.
I like it because it's an emotional watch, follows a great storyline and the plot twist is unexpected and heart warming.
Rating: 9.5/10

2. How to Train Your Dragon Series

This is an absolute comfort watch trilogy.
I personally love the second movie since it carries so much emotion. There are various moments where you're rooting for the main character -Hiccup and his dragon Toothless, to win and persevere.
Rating: 10/10

3. The Summer Hikaru Died

This is an Anime adaptation of a Manga with the same name.
It's a horror Anime combined with themes of Slice of Life following the story of two childhood friends who navigate their life around an unfortunate event where one of them- Hikaru, dies and comes back as a demonic entity.
The show is fairly new to Netflix and can be watched there as of the year 2025.
Rating: 9/10
